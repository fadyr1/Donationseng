Hand2Give

The site allows individuals to donate basic needs, such as clothes, food, etc., to those in need. Those in need can display their needs on the site, while donors can view these needs and provide the necessary assistance. The site also allows users to communicate directly to arrange the donation and delivery process. This system aims to build an interdependent society that supports each other and enhances the values ​​of cooperation and empathy among its members.
